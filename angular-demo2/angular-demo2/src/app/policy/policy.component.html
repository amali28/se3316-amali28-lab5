Description of workflow for Implementing DMCA notice & Takedown Policy

The DMCA provides a safe harbor for service providers that host user-generated content. Since even a single claim of copyright infringement can carry statutory damages of up to $150,000, the possibility of being held liable for user-generated content could be very harmful for service providers.

Instructions for using tools provided for impplementing the DMCA Notice and Takedown Policy

To use the tools the DCMA notice, click get policies below. You can then edit the text box and save policies. Please be advised that once you make a change it is accessible by the public.
